About Me

Welcome to my world! I’m Kaden Clegg, a passionate, ambitious, and creative individual striving to make an impact in every endeavor I pursue. Whether it's leading a team, innovating with AI, or capturing life’s beauty through a camera lens, I believe in doing it with heart and determination.

My Journey

Growing up, I always sought opportunities to lead, create, and excel. By the age of 13, I had already achieved my Eagle Scout award—a testament to my dedication and determination. Since then, I’ve ventured into business management, entrepreneurship, and AI innovation.

From managing Swig locations at the Delta Center to helping open and operate Crumbl franchises and Poke Bowl stores, my professional journey has been as diverse as it has been fulfilling. I am currently pursuing a degree in Entrepreneurship at BYU’s prestigious Marriott School of Business, where I continue to hone my skills and explore new opportunities.

What Sets Me Apart

  • Passionate about leadership and mentoring others.
  • Fluent in leveraging AI tools for problem-solving and efficiency.
  • Skilled in business operations, customer engagement, and marketing strategies.
  • Creative with a keen eye for photography and design.
  • Adaptable, resourceful, and always eager to learn.

Fun Facts

I love exploring new places, diving into creative projects, and keeping up with the latest trends in tech and business. In my free time, you’ll find me capturing scenic landscapes, jamming out to my favorite playlists, or brainstorming new ideas to bring to life.
